Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide additional information for your profile, such as a profile picture, bio, or location.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you use our service, including the songs you listen to, playlists you create, and your interactions with other users.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the devices you use to access our service, including hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Location Information: With your permission, we may collect and process information about your precise location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Develop new services and features
  • Personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Analyze and understand how our services are used

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of Echofy except in the following cases:

  • With your consent
  • With our trusted business partners who assist us in operating our service
  • For legal reasons, such as to comply with laws or respond to legal requests

4. Data Security

We work hard to protect our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information we hold. In particular:

  • We encrypt our services using SSL
  • We review our information collection, storage, and processing practices
  • We restrict access to personal information to Echofy employees, contractors, and agents who need to know that information

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your account and personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your information
  • Export your data

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may change this privacy policy from time to time. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice.
